Boat Scrub Windows (UK) v1.0

Planning dataset for UK boat maintenance teams who schedule scrubbing around tidal suitability windows.

Version: v1.0

Last updated: 2026-04-15

License: CC BY 4.0

Downloads: JSON · CSV

Schema

FieldTypeDescription
station_idstringUnique station identifier used by the app
station_namestringStation display name
country_regionstringUK country or region
latnumberLatitude in decimal degrees
lonnumberLongitude in decimal degrees
mhws_mnumberMean high water springs (m)
mhwn_mnumberMean high water neaps (m)
mlwn_mnumberMean low water neaps (m)
mlws_mnumberMean low water springs (m)
recommended_scrub_windowstringSuggested local time window for planning

Methodology

Values are derived from BoatScrubCalendar station profiles and modelled tide suitability heuristics intended for maintenance planning workflows. This dataset is not a navigation safety source.
